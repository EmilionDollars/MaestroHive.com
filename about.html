About the hive

We are
MaestroHive.

(01) Who we are

Built for
brilliance.

MaestroHive is a full-service creative technology agency founded in Lagos in 2023. We bring together media planning, communications, brand strategy, content creation, web and mobile development under one roof — one hive, one rhythm.

We don't believe in siloed agencies or half-measures. The world's most compelling brands are built by teams that can think in strategy and execute in craft simultaneously. That's why we built MaestroHive differently.

Our name says it all. A maestro orchestrates. A hive collaborates. We do both — bringing discipline, creativity and technology to bear in perfect concert for every client we partner with.

(02) What we believe

Our philosophy.

A brand is a living organism. It grows, evolves, speaks, listens — and when treated right, it hums with a frequency that no competitor can replicate.
◆ 01

Strategy first, always.

Great creative is meaningless without the right strategic foundation. We start by understanding your market, your audience and your ambition.

◆ 02

Craft is non-negotiable.

We obsess over details. Every pixel, every word, every frame is considered. Quality is not a service tier — it is the only standard we operate at.

◆ 03

Integration creates impact.

When media, brand, content and tech are designed to work together, the result is exponentially more powerful than any one discipline alone.

◆ 04

Partnerships, not projects.

The best work happens in long relationships built on trust. We invest in understanding your business as deeply as you do.

(03) What we do

Six disciplines,
one team.

Media Planning & Buying

Audience-first media strategy across broadcast, digital, outdoor and experiential. We buy smart and optimise relentlessly.

Communications & PR

Narrative strategy, media relations, stakeholder engagement and crisis comms. The stories your brand needs the world to hear.

Brand Management

Brand strategy, identity systems, verbal identity and governance. Living brands that are clear, connected and built to last.

Content & Creative

Editorial, film, motion, photography and social content. Stories shaped by strategy, shot with taste.

Web Design & Development

Marketing sites, editorial platforms and e-commerce. Hand-crafted design, meticulous engineering, zero compromise.

Mobile App Development

iOS, Android and cross-platform apps. Product thinking, design systems, native performance. Apps users love opening.

(04) Our journey

How the hive
came to be.

2023

Founded in Lagos

MaestroHive was established with a clear mission: to give growing African and global brands access to world-class creative and communications expertise under one roof.

2023

First clients onboarded

We partnered with BrandsandIdeas, ITG Africa and MyLenZ.app — delivering media, brand sound, voiceover production and digital presence work that set the tone for everything we build.

2024

Expanding the swarm

Growing our capabilities across web development, mobile and creative production — and deepening partnerships with clients across Nigeria and beyond.

2025

The hive is humming

Today we are a full-service creative technology hive, serving forward-looking companies who refuse to be invisible. The best chapter is the one we're writing now.
